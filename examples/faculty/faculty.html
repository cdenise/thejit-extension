Force Directed Static Graph

The nodes in the graph are professors at CSAIL,MIT. There is an edge between two professors if they have offices in the same floor. Shape coders have been used to differenciate between professors of Color coders have been used to differentiate professors in various floors.

You can zoom and pan the visualization by scrolling and dragging.

You can change node positions by dragging the nodes around.
